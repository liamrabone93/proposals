01 · From our conversation

What we heard from you.

A quick recap of Urban Blue as we understood it — so you can check we've got the picture right before we talk about where Milo fits.

01 / ORIGIN
Born from watching teams make avoidable mistakes.

A fish farm in Germany showed you how human error causes operational failures. Urban Blue exists to help teams make fewer of them — anywhere more than two people run a repetitive process.

02 / PLATFORM
Smart Connect already does the heavy lifting.

It connects to any data source, records every measurement, and captures the team's knowledge through checklists and routines. Measurements plus everyday work, combined into context.

03 / PHILOSOPHY
AI as chief of staff, never in control.

No AI touching the active systems. It responds to the team's needs and advises — a reliable helping hand, not an autopilot. Milo is built the same way: it reads and answers, it never acts.

04 / THE REAL PROBLEM
Too many indexes, all technically right.

Conflicting signals leave people guessing — one says go left, another go right, so they split the difference and both are wrong. The value is condensation: evaluating what actually matters today.

05 / TRUST
Proven where it's hardest: research & premium labs.

Max Planck Institute Frankfurt, premium German labs, fruit growers in France, a fish farm in Ireland. These are demanding, security-sensitive environments — and the platform holds up.

06 / THE DREAM
Teams that don't have to look at the table.

In your words: the new insight is the simple message. "This is how it is, you don't have to care about all these data points anymore." That's exactly the experience Milo is designed to deliver.

04 · How it fits, and where it runs

An answer layer on top, on your infrastructure.

Milo sits above Smart Connect and your situation database. It reads the context you've already built and answers questions. It never controls a system, and for the ongoing service it lives entirely on your servers.

How a question gets answered

A team member asks in plain language. Milo reads the live measurements and the team's captured knowledge, weighs what matters, and replies with a simple answer — not a wall of indexes.

Where it runs

The POC runs in AWS Frankfurt — data stays in that environment, never touching a third-party model. For the ongoing service, Milo is deployed onto your own Linux servers in Switzerland. Zero-cloud, as you require.
